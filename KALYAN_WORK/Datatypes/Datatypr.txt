Datatypes in python:

1. Integer (int) :
Whole numbers (positive, negative, or zero).
example: a = 10

2.Floating Point (float)
Numbers with decimals.
example: a = 2.5

3.Complex (complex)
Numbers with a real and imaginary part.
example : z = 2 + 3j

4.String
Sequence of characters (text).
example: name = "Kalyan"

5.Boolean (bool)
Represents truth values True or False.
example: bool = True
